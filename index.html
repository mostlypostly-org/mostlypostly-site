For Stylists

Text a photo. Review the caption. You're done.

No app to download. No login. No caption to write. MostlyPostly handles everything after the snap — and gives you a simple web preview to tweak before anything goes live.

1

Text a photo — that's the whole job

Between clients, snap and text the photo to your salon's number. Add a quick note if you want — service name, client detail, a vibe — or just send the photo.

2

Get a preview link in seconds

AI writes a full caption — tone, hashtags, booking CTA, your @handle — and texts you a link to review it. No app to install, just a tap on the link.

3

Review, edit, or regenerate on the preview page

The preview page shows your photo and caption. Add a note like "make it shorter" or "mention it's her first visit" and regenerate with one tap. Or submit it as-is.

4

Submit — it routes to your manager automatically

Hit Submit on the preview page and it's done. If your salon requires manager sign-off, it goes to the approval queue. Once approved, it publishes automatically within your salon's posting window. Reply APPROVE via SMS to skip the preview and submit instantly.

Security

Your clients trust you. You can trust us.

Security isn't a checkbox at MostlyPostly — it's part of the architecture. We built it the way we'd want our own data handled.

🛡️

Multi-Factor Authentication

Manager logins require email + password with SMS-verified reset codes. Every session is tied to a confirmed phone number.

📱

Phone-Verified Stylists

Only registered phone numbers can submit photos. Rogue or spoofed submissions are impossible by design.

🏢

Isolated Salon Data

Cross-tenant access is architecturally impossible. Stylists see only their posts; managers see only their salon.

🔏

Signed Webhooks

All Twilio messages are verified via cryptographic signatures — only legitimate stylist messages can trigger posts.

☁️

Enterprise Infrastructure

Auto HTTPS, DDoS protection, zero direct database exposure. Same stack trusted by serious SaaS businesses.

⏱️

Time-Limited Tokens

Password reset links expire in 45 minutes. Login session tokens expire in 7 days. No stale access.

🔐

bcrypt Password Hashing

All passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt rounds. Plain-text passwords are never stored or logged.

🕵️

Privacy First

We don't sell data or mine client info. Photos are used only to generate your post — never shared with third parties.
