How It Works

From your brand desk to salon feeds — automatically.

The MostlyPostly Vendor Repository is a curated content library maintained by professional beauty brands. Salons on the Pro plan opt in and choose which vendor feeds to enable. From there, approved campaign posts flow seamlessly into their existing scheduling queue.

1

You submit a campaign

Your marketing team uploads campaign assets — product photos, copy direction, hashtag sets, expiration dates, and posting frequency — to the MostlyPostly Vendor Repository.

2

Salons opt in

Pro-plan salons toggle your brand feed on inside their MostlyPostly settings. Each salon chooses which vendor partnerships they want active. No unwanted content, ever.

3

AI adapts to each salon

MostlyPostly's AI takes your brand direction and adapts the caption to each salon's unique tone of voice, hashtag set, and CTA — so every post feels local and authentic, not copy-pasted.

4

Posts go live on schedule

Campaigns are distributed according to your defined frequency and expiration rules. Posts respect each salon's posting window — never flooding feeds, never posting after the campaign ends.

Repository Data

Everything you bring. We handle the rest.

The Vendor Repository accepts rich campaign data, so posts come out accurate, well-written, and timed correctly. Here's what each campaign entry supports.

🖼️

Product Photos

Upload high-resolution, brand-approved product imagery. MostlyPostly attaches the correct photo to each distributed post automatically.

📝

Product Name & Description

Full product name, category, key ingredients or benefits, and usage context. Used by the AI to generate accurate, benefit-driven copy that educates and converts.

📅

Campaign Window & Expiration

Set start dates, end dates, and expiration rules. Posts will never be distributed after a campaign ends — seasonal launches, limited-time offers, and event windows are respected automatically.

🔁

Posting Frequency

Define how often each salon can post about your campaign — once a week, twice a month, or tied to launch windows. Your reach is controlled, not random.

#️⃣

Brand Hashtags

Your proprietary campaign hashtags are blended with each salon's local set — ensuring brand-level discoverability without overriding the salon's local SEO strategy.

🎨

Tone & Messaging Direction

Provide copy direction, key messages, and words to avoid. The AI follows your guardrails while still adapting to each salon's individual tone — premium, playful, educational, or bold.

🔗

CTA & Booking Instructions

Specify the action you want clients to take — book a service, ask about the product, or learn more. MostlyPostly weaves the CTA into each post naturally, linked to the salon's own booking page.

📊

Campaign Analytics

Track reach, engagement, and post volume across every participating salon. Understand which markets are most active and where your campaign is resonating — without chasing individual salons for data.

🏷️

Service & Retail Pairing

Tie your product to specific services (e.g., "pairs with color correction" or "recommended for balayage maintenance"). Helps generate content that drives both retail sales and service bookings simultaneously.
