This Privacy Policy explains how MostlyPostly (“MostlyPostly,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use our services (the “Service”). By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1) Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: name, mobile phone number, role, business/salon name.
  • Message & Media Content: text, images, captions, approvals, and related workflow messages sent to or from the Service.
  • Technical & Usage Data: timestamps, IP address, device and app info, message metadata, logs, and interaction data.
  • Account/Settings Data: preferences (e.g., tone/hashtags), routing/approval settings, and platform tokens/IDs you connect (e.g., Facebook/Instagram Page or IG User IDs).

Note: If you connect third-party accounts (e.g., Facebook/Instagram), we may receive identifiers and permissions necessary to post content you approve.

2) How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain the Service (AI caption generation, previews, approvals, posting to connected pages/accounts).

  • Improve, secure, and troubleshoot the Service (analytics, logging, fraud and abuse prevention).
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.
  • Communicate with you about service changes, security, and support.
  • Enable approved posting, analytics, and account management for connected Facebook Pages and Instagram Business accounts, in accordance with Meta’s Platform Policies.

SMS is operational/transactional only. We do not send promotional SMS.
