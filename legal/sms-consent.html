What is MostlyPostly?

MostlyPostly is an AI assistant that helps salon teams create, preview, and approve social media posts via text/chat. By providing your mobile number to MostlyPostly (e.g., during onboarding or by messaging our service number), you consent to receive automated text messages related to caption/post creation, approvals, and operational updates for your salon.

Message Purpose (Transactional Only)

Messages are transactional, used only for:

  • Sending AI-generated captions or post previews
  • Confirming post approvals, denials, or edits
  • System notifications related to your use of MostlyPostly

We do not send unsolicited marketing messages.

Frequency & Rates

  • Message frequency varies based on your usage and team activity.
  • Message & data rates may apply per your mobile plan.

Privacy Commitment

  • We do not sell, rent, or share mobile subscriber information for marketing or promotional purposes.
  • Mobile information may be shared only with service providers acting on our behalf under confidentiality obligations.
  • Text STOP to opt out; text HELP for help.

See also: Privacy Policy • Terms of Service

Opt-In

By providing your mobile number or replying "AGREE" (or "YES") via text, you consent to receive automated SMS through the MostlyPostly platform for post approvals, previews, and operational updates. Msg & data rates may apply. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Opt-Out / Help

  • Text STOP to stop receiving messages.
  • Text HELP for assistance.
  • To re-enroll, text START.
